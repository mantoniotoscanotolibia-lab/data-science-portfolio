# Finance Projects

This folder contains Python projects focused on quantitative finance, financial modeling, and risk analytics.

These projects are designed to demonstrate practical applications of data science in financial decision-making, including pricing, simulation, portfolio analysis, and credit risk evaluation.

---

## Projects

### Natural Gas Trading Strategy with Forecasting and Backtesting
A quantitative trading project that models natural gas prices using time series forecasting, generates trading signals, and evaluates performance through backtesting against a passive benchmark.

**Main topics:** forecasting, trading signals, backtesting, performance evaluation

---

### Black-Scholes Option Pricing
Implementation of the Black-Scholes model to estimate the theoretical price of European options based on asset price, volatility, interest rates, and time to maturity.

**Main topics:** derivatives pricing, financial mathematics, option valuation

---

### Monte Carlo Stock Simulation
Simulation model that generates multiple possible future stock price paths using geometric Brownian motion to represent uncertainty in financial markets.

**Main topics:** stochastic processes, simulation, stock price modeling

---

### Portfolio Optimization (Markowitz)
A portfolio optimization model based on modern portfolio theory, exploring the relationship between expected return and risk through diversification.

**Main topics:** portfolio theory, risk-return tradeoff, efficient frontier

---

### Credit Risk Probability of Default Model
A machine learning-based credit risk project that estimates borrower probability of default (PD) and supports expected loss analysis.

**Main topics:** credit risk, classification, probability of default

---

### FICO Credit Rating Model
A model that maps FICO scores into credit risk categories using score segmentation techniques.

**Main topics:** credit scoring, rating classification, risk segmentation
